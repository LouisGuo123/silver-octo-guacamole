Authors' Resources

Per the greenlighting policy, first-time authors must get concepts reviewed in the Ideas Critique Forum or IRC chatrooms before creating a feedback thread in the Drafts and Critique forum. Draft critique can still be sought via IRC and PM without greenlights.
When you have a draft that you know for sure the audience will like, bring it to Drafts Critique Forum. Please note that the forums move much slower than the chat, and the draft forum moves slower than the ideas forum!
The mainsite-run IRC chatrooms are the most reliable source of real-time critique discussion.

ModestGreen - Test Page 2
rating: 0+x

Item #: SCP-XXXX

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-XXXX is to be monitored at all times to ensure its influence doesn't extend beyond its current abilitycapability. No other containment procedures are necessaryrequired at this time.

Description: SCP-XXXX is an anomolous phenomenon associated with the Foundation's articlereport written on it. The anomoly causes text to occasionally be replacedmodified, usually with minor changes. Such changes include replacing words with synonyms, restructuring sentences, and adding newnovel adjectives. Rarely, entirely new content is appended to the article. Anomolous edits can identified through the edit log of the article, where they are markedlisted as unauthorized.

The anomoly's behaviour has changed a number of times. The first notable change occured when Dr. Kaur replaced all page content with the article on SCP-6969, and the second occured when the anomoly was redesignated from SCP-4420 to SCP-XXXX.

Addendum XXXX-01: The article was originally a placeholder, created on 09/03/2022. Unauthorized edits were discovered on 02/04/2023, by Dr. Kaur when writing about SCP-6969.

Addendum XXXX-02: As requested by Dr. imtoolazy and Dr. imstillverylazy, below is a list of all known anomolous edits:

  • 01/01/2023 00:00 - Add "Hello World!" at top of page
  • 01/08/2023 02:41 - Add "\n<script>alert("Hello World!")</script>" after "Hello, World!"
  • 01/08/2023 02:43 - Remove "Hello World!", remove "<script>alert("Hello World!")</script>", add "<p>Hello World!</p>"
  • 01/08/2023 02:44 - Remove "<p>Hello World!</p>"
  • 01/19/2023 13:37 - Add "[[div]]\nHello, World!\n[[/div]]"
  • 01/19/2023 13:56 - Add "\n* Hello World!\n* Hello, World!\n* Hello World!"
  • 01/20/2023 07:14 - Insert "[[module CSS]]\n.hello-world {\ncolor: red;\n}\n[[/module]]\n" at top of page, insert "class=\"hello-world\"" at [[div << line 1
  • 01/31/2023 04:47 - Delete "[[module CSS]]\n.hello-world {\ncolor: red;\n}\n[[/module]]\n[[div class=\"hello-world\"]]\nHello, World!\n[[/div]]\n* Hello World!\n* Hello World!\n* Hello, World!", add "[[html]]<p>Hello, World!</p>[[/html]]"
